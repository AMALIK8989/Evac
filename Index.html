Why Evac?

In the existing system, Vaccines are provided for infants and children under teenage by various aspects such as by attending school manually, leading to a chance that some may miss out on vaccination, which can result in loss of life or disease. The proposed solution is to change this manual system into an e-vaccination system.

Our Work

We aim to transform the vaccination process by providing an efficient and convenient platform for parents, hospitals, and administrators. By streamlining the process, we ensure that every child receives timely vaccinations, thus contributing to public health.

What We Offer

We offer a comprehensive e-vaccination system that includes features for administrators, parents, and hospitals. Our platform ensures efficient scheduling, easy access to vaccination records, and seamless communication between all stakeholders.

About Us

Evac was created with the mission to revolutionize the vaccination process and improve public health outcomes. Our team is dedicated to providing an innovative solution that ensures every child receives timely vaccinations, thereby safeguarding their health and well-being.
